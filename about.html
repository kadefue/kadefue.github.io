Biography

Dr. Kadeghe "Kade" Goodluck Fue is a distinguished professional in the field of Digital and Data-Driven Agriculture and Natural Resource Management. His expertise spans vital areas such as Precision Agriculture, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Vision, Agricultural Robotics, Agricultural Data Science, and Earth Data Science.

With more than 14 years of professional experience, Dr. Fue specializes in full-stack development, integrating hardware and software systems. He is known for creating innovative algorithms that enhance autonomy and apply AI in the agricultural and natural resource domains.

He serves as a senior lecturer, consulting engineer, and researcher at the Department of Agricultural Engineering at the School of Engineering and Technology at Sokoine University of Agriculture (SUA). He is also the Principal Investigator at the Electronics and Precision Agriculture Lab (EPAL) and coordinator for the AIMS unit in the office of the deputy vice-chancellor for academic, research, and consultancy.
