Welcome

Welcome to the GitHub site for Dr Kadeghe "Kade" Goodluck Fue. This site provides an overview of my work in Digital and Data-Driven Agriculture, Precision Agriculture, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Vision, Agricultural Robotics, Agricultural Data Science and Earth Data Science.

I am a senior lecturer, consulting engineer, and researcher at the Department of Agricultural Engineering, Sokoine University of Agriculture (SUA), and Principal Investigator at the Electronics and Precision Agriculture Lab (EPAL).
